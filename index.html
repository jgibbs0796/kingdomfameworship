Kingdom Fame Worship

About Us

Kingdom Fame Worship is a group of several worship leaders from different communities in Mississippi. Led by Anse Rigby, they seek to spread the message of Christ through song. Kingdom Fame Worhship has led worship for many different events and for many different denominations. If you would like Kingdom Fame Worship to lead worship at your next event, please contact us.


Pray

We are called to be disciples. To help us in continuing our mission, we ask that you pray for us and for those we lead in worship. Pray that God will use us as a means to reach those who need Him. Pray for comfort for those that are hurting. Pray for peace for those who are not at peace. Pray for open eyes and open hearts. Pray.
